Stored On Chain

Pepes are generated and stored entirely on-chain and minted as ERC404 tokens. Unlike regular NFTs, there is no IPFS or image link. This ensures that your art can never be tampered with or lost.

Limited Supply

With the total supply being 88 tokens, we will have a maximum of only 88 Pepe NFTs circulating, generated randomly by a smart contract at the moment of minting.

"Fractions" Burned

Every time a wallet has more than 1 $OCP404 token a Pepe is minted and sent to their wallet. If that wallet has less than 1 $OCP404 that Pepe is burnt.

SVGs Stored On-Chain

OnChainPepe employs a novel approach by storing SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) data of each NFT part directly on the Ethereum blockchain. This means that unlike many NFT projects that store their images on external servers or IPFS, OnChainPepe NFTs are fully on-chain.

100% Decentralized

On-chain storage does not only ensure permanence and tamper-proofing but also aligns with the ethos of decentralization in the crypto space. The ERC404 standard, utilized by the project, enhances that by allowing for complex interactions and data storage directly within the smart contract.

Finite Max Supply

With the total supply being 88 tokens, we will have a maximum of only 88 Pepe NFTs circulating, generated randomly by a smart contract at the moment of minting.
We have implemented an innovative mechanism of minting and burning in ERC404 keeping the dual-token system balanced.

Tokenomics and Minting

When tokens are transferred, if the amount was >= 1, a new NFT minted as an ERC-721 token. This ensures that the minting process is direct1y 1inked to the activity w1thin the ecosystem, creating a dynam1c and interactive minting experience.
If a fraction of a token is transferred (not discrete - e.g. 1.5 tokens), 1 token wi11 be minted and transferred as an ERC-721 NFT, while the remaining 0.5 token will be transferred as an ERC-20 token.

Tokens Burned

There cannot be fractions of the 88 Pepe NFTs circulating or held in wallets.
The moment an NFT holder sells or transfers a fraction of the token they hold, if the remaining amount is not a whole number, the remaining fraction of the NFT is burned and a monetary equivalent of the burned token gets stored in an ERC20 token to preserve value.

Rarity Rankings

The rarity of each NFT within the OnChainPepe collection is determined by specific attributes, including skin, lip, and shirt colors. This creates a detailed rarity framework, ensuring that each NFT is unique and has its own value within the ecosystem.
